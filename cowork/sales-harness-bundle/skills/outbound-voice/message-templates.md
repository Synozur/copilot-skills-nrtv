# Message Templates

Frames only. The Composer rewrites every line in firm voice using `voice-dna.md`. If a draft reads like the template verbatim, the voice extraction is too thin.

## Subject lines

For the opener:
- A specific noun from the hook + 3 words. "Your post on agent evals"
- Never start with "Quick" or end with "?"
- 4–8 words

For follow-ups:
- Reply on the same thread (no new subject).

## Opener (first touch)

```
{First name}, saw {one specific signal from ## Hook — post, funding,
hire, talk — with the date}.

{One-sentence reaction tied to the [YOUR_FIRM] ICP pain. Specific. No
generic "this resonates with what we do" language.}

Worth a 10-minute call if {a specific outcome the prospect would care
about}?
```

Three lines. Specific signal. Reaction. Specific ask. No filler.

## Follow-up (day 3 if no reply)

```
{First name}, no pressure on the previous note.

The reason I asked: {one-sentence specific reason tied to the same
signal as the opener, not a new pitch}.

If it's not a fit, just say so and I'll get out of your way.
```

The "get out of your way" close converts because it signals respect and lack of neediness. It is also true — if it's not a fit, do not waste your time.

## Backup (day 7 if no reply, on the same thread)

```
{First name}, last try on this one. {Reference to where the original
signal came from}.

{One-sentence punchier restatement of the ask.}

If now is not the right time, happy to resurface in {a specific
trigger — next quarter, or when {company} {does the thing}}.
```

Catches prospects who simply missed the first two. The "resurface in {trigger}" close gives them a graceful out and you a calendared follow-up.

## Reply to "interested" or "tell me more"

```
{First name}, glad it landed. Three things to know:

- {one sentence on what [YOUR_FIRM] actually does, in voice}
- {one sentence on a recent comparable outcome}
- {one sentence with the calendar link or 3 proposed slots}

Anything else worth knowing before we talk?
```

## Reply to "not now" / objection

```
{First name}, fair. {Brief acknowledgement that does not argue.}

The reason I keep an eye on {company} is {one specific reason — not
"because you're a leader" or any flattery}.

I'll resurface in {trigger}. If anything changes on your end, the easiest
thing is to just reply to this thread.
```

## Reply to a question

The Composer answers the question in voice in 2–4 sentences, then asks one clarifying question that moves toward a meeting. No marketing-deck language. No bullet lists in the reply unless the prospect used bullets.
