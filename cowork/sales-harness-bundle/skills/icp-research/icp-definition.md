## Signals that decrease fit

- Headcount below your minimum (typically < 50–150 depending on offering)
- Headcount above your maximum (enterprise procurement models that exclude boutiques)
- Bootstrapped with no observable growth signal
- Industry on the exclusion list (see `disqualifiers.md`)
- No public web presence beyond a landing page
- Buyer expectation mismatch (e.g. they want implementation, you advise)
- Already engaged a competitor for the exact same scope in the last 12 months

## Scoring rubric

- **9–10**: Multiple high-value signals in the last 14 days, role and company size dead-center, clear ICP fit. Lead today.
- **7–8**: One strong signal (new exec, funding event, public-strategy post), good fit on role and size. Lead this week.
- **5–6**: General fit on size + role + industry, no recent signal. Consider for nurture, not cold outreach.
- **3–4**: Marginal fit — wrong role seniority, or company size at the edges of the range. Skip for now.
- **1–2**: Wrong role, wrong size, or wrong industry. `disqualified`.
- **0**: Hits a hard disqualifier from `disqualifiers.md`.
