---
name: Pre-Commitment Capacity Check
description: Before I agree to something new, review my actual workload and
  flag capacity risks across calendar, email, and Teams.
---
1. Review my calendar for the next 30 days and note meetings, travel,
   and blocked time.
2. Scan my sent emails from the last two weeks for explicit commitments
   (phrases like "I'll handle", "I'll send", "I'll review").
3. Review recent Teams chats for open asks or in‑progress discussions
   likely to convert into work.
4. Summarize my current obligation load in plain language, separating:
   - Committed work
   - Likely incoming work
5. Flag any weeks that are already at high capacity.
6. Give me a one‑line recommendation: good time to add something, or not.
